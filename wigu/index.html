Wigu's Design Philosophy

WIGU is an acronym which stands for When I Grow Up. We're all about exploring what you want to be when you grow up, or what you wanted to be when you were growing up. It is this desire that has inspired our vision and design philosophy.

Our Games

Doctor Life: Be a Doctor!

Don't let the cuteness fool you! Doctor Life is a challenging casual simulation game where you take on the role of a Doctor! Set in a charming little city with wacky patients, you must grow your humble clinic into a multistory hospital! You will meet patients along your journey and help them by diagnosing and treating their real life diseases with actual treatments.


Beware though! There is an evil tycoon out to buy the land your hospital is on. Earn awards for hospital excellence to save your hospital from his grubby hands.

Doctor Life for Kids!

Doctor Life for Kids is a challenging educational simulation and time management game where you learn what it takes to be a Doctor. Have fun while learning the symptoms of diseases and how to treat them!


Set in a charming little city with wacky patients, you must grow your humble clinic into a multistory hospital! You will meet patients along your journey and help them by diagnosing and treating their diseases with actual treatments. Beware though! There is an evil tycoon out to buy the land your hospital is on. Earn awards for hospital excellence to save your hospital from his grubby hands.

Canine Life: Woof Woof!

Become an ace dog trainer in Canine Life!


With over 50 types of dogs to call your own, encounter exotic dogs such as the Afghan Hound, the Bedlington Terrier and the Saluki! Train them and collect their info cards to learn more about these beautiful animals. As you adopt more dogs, unlock more upgrades for your training center to make it a center of awesomeness. While doing that, you must care for your stars, train them, and compete with them in competitions as you take on tougher and tougher competitors.


Make a name for yourself and claim the crown as the best dog trainer in all the land.
